  • Performance Tracking: Monitor expectancy changes as you implement AI recommendations
  • Continuous Validation: Regularly review and update insights as market conditions change

FAQs

How much historical data do I need for AI insights?
Minimum 100-200 trades per setup for meaningful patterns. More data improves accuracy and reduces false positives.
Can AI insights work for different futures contracts?
Yes, but insights are most valuable when analyzed separately for each contract due to different characteristics.
How often should I update my AI insights?
Monthly reviews work well for most traders. Update more frequently during major market regime changes.
What if AI insights contradict my existing strategy?
Test insights carefully in paper trading. AI may reveal optimization opportunities within your existing approach.
How do I know if AI insights are actually improving expectancy?
Track expectancy metrics before and after implementation. Look for consistent improvements over multiple weeks.
